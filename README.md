## The Real Cost (Beyond the Membership Fee)

Here's what people don't always mention upfront:

- **Meeting room overages:** After you use your credits, expect $25-50/hour
- **Printing beyond your allowance:** $0.10-0.25 per page
- **Guest passes:** $29-59 per guest per day (adds up for client meetings)
- **Parking:** Usually not included; downtown rates can be $200-400/month

So while the All Access Plus membership is $279-339/month, your actual monthly cost might be closer to $400-500 if you're using the space regularly for client meetings and need extra meeting room time.

## Who Should Actually Use WeWork

**It Makes Sense For:**
- Remote workers wanting professional space a few days per week
- Startups that need to scale up or down quickly
- Freelancers who want to separate work from home
- Business travelers needing consistent workspace globally
- Small teams (2-10 people) avoiding long office leases

**Skip It If:**
- You're extremely budget-conscious (local coworking spaces might be cheaper)
- You need guaranteed quiet workspace daily
- You rarely leave your home office
- You're in a city with one or two WeWork locations (limited options if your spot is full)
